## Role Summary

Food prep volunteers help prepare food for the party before the event starts.

## When

This shift is a few hours of work in the afternoon.

## Typical Tasks

- Slice vegetables.
- Make quiches.
- Follow guidance from the kitchen lead. We will guide and help you.

## Questions

Check in with the kitchen lead or event organizer if anything is unclear.
