## Role Summary

Roadies help load and unload the truck and move boxes. You don't need to be very strong, and no gear or prior experience is required.

## When

Roadies are available around noon. They can show up in Brussels to help load and unload the truck. They will be contacted with specific times and places.

## Typical Tasks

- Pick up and carry boxes.
- Load and unload the truck.
- Move items as directed by the lead.

## Questions

Check in with the roadie lead or event organizer if anything is unclear.
